* Disclaimer:

This project does not claim definitive decipherment of the Etruscan language.
All parsing systems, reconstruction models, and probabilistic outputs are intended as computational and philological research tools designed to evaluate grammatical structure, morphological behavior, and corpus-wide linguistic relationships.
The project prioritizes methodological transparency, statistical rigour, and reproducible analysis over speculative interpretation.
